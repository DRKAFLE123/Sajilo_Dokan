SAJILO DOKAN SAAS PLATFORM - MASTER IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
=====================================================

1. ARCHITECTURAL OVERVIEW
-------------------------
The platform is divided into three distinct ecosystems to maintain clean permissions:
- Super Admin Dashboard: Platform owner's control center.
- Shop Owner Dashboard: Merchant's simplified ERP.
- Customer Dashboard: Buyer's personal account.

2. ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL (RBAC)
-----------------------------------
- Super Admin: Full control over platform, financials, and users.
- Moderator: Review shops, products, and handle KYC.
- Support Agent: Handle disputes and customer messages.
- Shop Owner: Full control over their own store and staff.
- Shop Staff: Limited access to orders/inventory (managed by owner).
- Customer: Personal account, orders, and reviews.

3. PHASE-WISE IMPLEMENTATION
----------------------------

PHASE 1: SAAS FOUNDATION (COMPLETED)
- Subscription Models: Multi-tier plan architecture (Free, Pro, Premium).
- Admin Shell: Modern sidebar layout with SD branding.
- KYC Hub: Identity verification workflow for shop owners.
- Plan Management: Dynamic pricing and feature list controls.

PHASE 2: ADVANCED CONTROL & MODERATION (COMPLETED)
- User Management: Search, filter, and suspend any user (Customer/Owner).
- Product Moderation: Mandatory review queue for new listings.
- Status Workflow: Draft -> Submitted -> Under Review -> Approved -> Live.
- Advanced Filtering: Accordion panels for price, location, and rating.

PHASE 2.1: PROMOTION & FEATURING (COMPLETED)
- Admin Controls: "Feature" toggle for any Shop or Product.
- Landing Page Slider: Dynamic "Featured" section scrolling right-to-left.
- Promotion Labels: Custom tags like "Sale", "Special Offer", or "Premium" managed by Admin.

PHASE 3: SHOP OWNER PROFESSIONAL SUITE (COMPLETED)
- Order Lifecycle: Flow management (Pending -> Packed -> Shipped -> Delivered).
- Store Analytics: Traffic sources, search terms, and conversion data.
- Shop Staff: Permissions system for owners to invite employees.
- Subscription Enforcement: Automatic product limits based on plan.
- Branding Controls: Store banner, description, and custom policy settings.

PHASE 4: FINANCIALS & SETTINGS
- Commission Settings: Set percentage per category (e.g., Electronics 8%, Fashion 12%).
- Payout System: Manual/Automatic payout requests for sellers.
- Global Settings: Feature toggles (Turn Chat/COD/Reviews ON/OFF globally).
- Branding: Master control for platform logos, theme colors, and email templates.

PHASE 5: CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE & NOTIFICATIONS
- Notification Center: Real-time web/push alerts for all roles.
- Dispute Center: System for handling returns, refunds, and complaints.
- Wishlist & Follow: Save products and follow favorite stores.
- Smart Search: Keyword-based unified search with ranking.

4. UI/UX STANDARDS
------------------
- Desktop: Left Sidebar + Top Search + Main Content.
- Mobile: Bottom Navigation Bar (optimized for shop owners on the go).
- Design: Clean, structured "Sajilo" style using Indigo/Gray palette.

5. ENTITY & WORKFLOW DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------
- Shop: Moderated entry (Draft to Live).
- Product: Moderated listing (Review queue).
- Order: Multi-step tracking (Customer <-> Seller <-> Admin).
- Subscription: Automated tiering (Upgrade/Downgrade logic).
